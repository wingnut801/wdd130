Utah

Utah is a western U.S. state known for its diverse landscapes, including red rock formations, vast deserts, and the snow-capped Rocky Mountains. It is home to five national parks and has a rich cultural heritage, with strong ties to Native American history and early Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints (Mormon) pioneers. Utah's capital, Salt Lake City, is a hub for outdoor recreation, arts, and industry.
